Hello World

First of all, thanks for reading.

I’ve been meaning to write a blog for a long time. I’ve even taken a couple of tries at it. However, it wasn’t up until recently that I feel I might have something useful to say to the World. Hence the title of this first post.

Hello World; I’ve been a long time listening, now it’s time for me to shout a bit.

In the following days I plan to write some stuff about myself (beginning at the typical personal introduction), my work and other (not so) interesting rants.

I will also polish as much as I can the details around here; typography and such, there’s a lot of small thingies to take care of.

Again, thanks for reading. I know I haven’t given you enough reasons to come back yet, but I’ll try my best to change that in a near future.

David Guijarro

Mamma was a designer, Pappa was a developer. So I'm a Devesigner, with the fate of bringing peace and understanding between the Pixels and the Bytes.
